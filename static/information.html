Why does InFact exist?

Media bias has been a huge issue in media consumption. Often, this bias results in limited perspectives, narrow-mindedness, and sometimes even misinformation.

Why aren't some sources viewable?

Certain sources (take Reuters for example) do not allow us to web scrape, which is how we obtain the article content. If you were to click for the corroborated version of these articles, they may result in an "Internal Server Error".
